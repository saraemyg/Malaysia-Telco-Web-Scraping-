International Roaming

FAQ
  1. What is International Roaming?

    International Roaming is a service where it allows you to use your mobile number to make and receive calls in
    countries or destinations while you are overseas.

  2. What should I do if I want to be able to make calls, send SMS or browse internet when I’m travelling
    overseas?

    You will need to pre-activate your roaming services before you travel overseas.

  3. How do I activate my International Roaming service?

    If you are a redONE customer for more than 6 months, you just need to call 1909 to speak to our Customer Care to
    activate your International Roaming service before you travel abroad.

    If you are a new redONE customer (less than 6 months), then you may be required to pay an additional RM300 deposit
    (refundable) to activate your International Roaming service. The additional deposit payment can be waived if you
    ported (MNP) your Postpaid number from another network, and you had been roaming previously.

  4. What are the steps to make calls or send SMS while I’m roaming?

    Just follow the simple instruction below:

    <International Access Code><Country Code><Area Code/Mobile Network Code><Mobile/Fixed
    Line Number>

    Example:
    To make a call to a Malaysian mobile number (e.g. 011 1073 3663), dial +601110733663

  5. How do I manually select a network operator from my mobile device?

    To manually select a network, please follow the steps below:

    iPhoneGo to:
    • Settings
    • Carrier
    • Automatic [OFF]
    • Select the correct preferred network
    AndroidGo to:
    • Menu
    • Settings
    • Wireless and Networks
    • Mobile Networks
    • Network Operator
    • Select the correct preferred network
    BlackBerryGo to:
    • Options
    • Networks and Connections
    • Mobile Network
    • Click on Network Selection Mode and select MANUAL. Under the Manual Network Selection, there are 3 options:
      1. Prompt – under this option customer will be prompted to switch when the manually selected network is out of coverage
      2. Yes – this option will allow user to switch to available network (Auto) when manually selected network is out of coverage
      3. No – user will not be having network coverage when the manually selected network is out of coverage.

    Note: Steps may vary from one mobile phone to another

  6. What are the charging rates for making calls, sending SMS or browsing the internet while roaming?

    The charges are based on the roaming country and operator you selected.

  7. Are there any Peak/Off-Peak hours to make any international call?

    No, the charging rates are standard depending on the country and operator you selected.

  8. Will I be charged for answering calls while I’m roaming?

    Yes, you will be charged if you answer any calls while you are roaming. The charges will vary according to the country of originating calls.

  9. Can I see the number of my caller on my mobile screen when I am roaming?

    Yes, you can.

  10. Who do I call in case of emergency such as lost SIM, lost phone, etc?

    You are advised to contact our Customer Care at +603 8689 7800 to suspend your line in order to avoid any unauthorised usages.

  11. Will I be charged if I call redONE Customer Care while roaming?

    Yes, you will be charged International Roaming service charges depending on the country and operator you selected.

  12. Are these International Roaming rates applicable to supplementary line users?

    Yes, these rates are applicable to supplementary line users with activated International Roaming service.

  13. Will I be notified of any changes in International Roaming rates?

    Changes in rates will be published in our website. Please check our website periodically to get the latest rates and promotions.

Daily Data Roaming

FAQ

Daily Data Roaming

    1. What is Daily Data Roaming?

      Daily Data Roaming allows subscribers to enjoy Internet access while travelling abroad.

Daily Data Roaming

  1. Do I need to log on to a certain network to enjoy the Daily Data Roaming?

    No, you do not need to log on to a preferred operator.

  2. Do I need to register or subscribe in order to enjoy the rates?

    You will be automatically activated upon data usage of more than RM0.10 in the eligible countries. Please ensure to switch ON Data Roaming on your device setting.

  3. Will I be informed about my activation?

    Yes, you will be informed via SMS upon activation and 100% usage utilization.

  4. What is Unlimited Daily Data Roaming?

    This service allows customers to access mobile data services when abroad using foreign mobile networks, without having to pay high data roaming charges. The cost is capped at a flat daily rate.

  5. What if the country that I am travelling to is not on the list of countries?

    Then standard data roaming rates will apply, which will be very expensive. Look for free WiFi hotspots instead.

  6. What is the charging block for Pay-Per-Use?

    The charging block is per 100 kb.

  7. If I’ve reached my daily quota, can I still enjoy the Internet while roaming without additional charges?

    Yes, you can continue browsing without any additional charges at reduced speed of up to 256 Kbps until your pass expires (FUP 700 MB).

  8. Can I unsubscribe from the Daily Data Roaming?

    No. The Daily Data Roaming will expire automatically within 24 hours. If you do not wish to continue, please turn off data roaming from your device settings.

  9. Can I travel to multiple countries using one subscription?

    You can use your Daily Data Roaming for 24 hours in the country you’re currently in. If you travel to another eligible country within that 24-hour window, a new Daily Data Roaming pass will be automatically activated for you, and RM39 will be charged.

  10. What are the eligible countries that are allocated with a 1 GB quota?

    Click the button provided on the page above to see the full list of the selected countries.

redROAM10

FAQ
  1. What is the new redROAM10?

    redROAM10 is a roaming add-on that gives you 10GB high-speed internet when you travel to Indonesia, Singapore, and Thailand for only RM10 per month.

  2. How long is this offer?

    This offer is effective from 1 October 2025

  3. Who is eligible to subscribe to the new redROAM10?

    redROAM10 Add-On is available for redONE Postpaid customers with a monthly commitment of RM38 and above.

  4. How can I sign up for these plans?

    Sign up in just a few taps with the redONE Postpaid 1App.

  5. How will I know when my activation is confirmed?

    You will receive an SMS notification once your pass has been successfully activated.

  6. Do I need to connect to a specific network to use redROAM10?

    No, you don’t need to manually select a network. Your device will automatically connect to the relevant participating operators.

  7. What happens if I fully utilize my quota?

    Once you’ve fully used up your 10GB high-speed data roaming quota, data roaming service will be slowed down up to 64Kbps. To continue enjoying data roaming, you may add the redROAM10 1-Time Add-On.

  8. Will I get notified when my redROAM10 is fully used?

    Yes, you will receive an SMS notification when you have used 100% of your data pass quota.

  9. Can I use one redROAM10 subscription across multiple countries?

    Yes. A single redROAM10 subscription can be used across all participating countries (Singapore, Indonesia, and Thailand) within the same billing cycle. For instance, if you travel to Singapore and Thailand within the same month, the redROAM10 pass activated in Singapore will work seamlessly in Thailand too.

  10. What if the country that I am travelling to other than Indonesia, Singapore or Thailand?

    redROAM10 is only valid in Singapore, Indonesia, and Thailand. If you enable data roaming in other countries, the standard Daily Data Roam rate of RM39 for 1GB will apply.

  11. Are there any charges for voice calls & SMS while roaming?

    redROAM10 is limited to data usage only. For voice calls & SMS, call diverted to voice & call forwarding while roaming, standard roaming charges for these services applies.

  12. What happens if I’m already charged RM39 for Daily Data Roam and then purchase redROAM10 on the same day?

    Both charges will apply. The RM39 Daily Data Roam fee is non-refundable, and your redROAM10 Add-On will take effect immediately upon successful subscription for the remaining validity period.

  13. What should I do if my roaming isn’t working after I purchase redROAM10?

    1. Ensure that Data Roaming is turn on in your phone settings.

    2. Restart your device to refresh the connection.

    3. Make sure you are within one of the supported countries (Singapore, Indonesia or Thailand).

    4. If the issue persists, manually select a participating network operator or contact redONE Customer Care for assistance.

  14. Any tips to avoid skyrocketing roaming bill?

    Here are some tips to help you manage your roaming costs:

    1. Turn off voicemail to avoid unnecessary charges

    2. Disable data roaming before expiry

    3. Limit heavy data usage while abroad

    4. Avoid replying to non-essential SMS

  15. Can I cancel my Monthly Recurring redROAM10 Add-On?

    Yes. You can cancel anytime via the redONE Customer Care. Once cancelled, the Add-On will not renew in the next billing cycle.

Prepaid Roaming Passes

FAQ
  1. Who can buy the pass?
    Customers with an active ULTRAplus25 or ULTRAplus35 RM35 plan are eligible to purchase the roaming pass.
  1. How is the pass activated?
    The pass price is deducted from your credit balance upon activation. Make sure your mobile line is active to complete the purchase successfully.
  1. Will my data be prorated?
    The full data quota applies once activated. Any unused data will expire at the end of the pass validity.
  1. Can I use any network overseas?
    There’s no need to manually log on to a preferred operator. The network selection will automatically connect you to the relevant participating operators.
  1. Is it valid in more than one country?
    Multi-country passes allow usage across all listed eligible countries.
  1. Can I use hotspot overseas?
    Yes, you can use the pass for data roaming and hotspot sharing.
  1. Will I get 5G access?
    Yes, 5G is automatically enabled once your pass is activated.
  1. When does my pass start and end?
    Your pass is valid from the time of activation for the selected duration. The full charge applies at activation, and any usage outside the validity period will incur standard roaming rates.
  1. What pass durations are available?
    You may choose from 3-day, 7-day, or 14-day passes — all valid for multi-country use.
  1. Can I buy another pass before the current one ends?
    Yes! redROAM passes are stackable. You can buy the same pass again even while your current one is still active.
  1. What happens if I have more than one active pass?
    The pass with the shortest validity and latest purchase time will take priority.
  1. What happens when my pass expires?
    Once your pass expires, data roaming will automatically stop to prevent additional charges.
  1. Will the pass renew automatically?
    The redROAM pass does not auto-renew — you’ll need to repurchase it to continue using roaming services.
  1. Can I use hotspot while roaming?
    Yes, redROAM passes cover all data roaming services, including hotspot usage.
  1. Can I share my data with others?
    Yes, you can share your high-speed data with other users or devices, but not with modems.
  1. Do I get 5G access with redROAM?
    Yes, 5G is automatically enabled once your redROAM pass is activated.
  2. Can I make calls using the pass?
    redROAM passes are for data use only. Any calls made will be charged separately at Pay-Per-Use (PPU) rates.
