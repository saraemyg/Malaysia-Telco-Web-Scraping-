Frequently asked questions

How can I avoid unexpected roaming charges?

Get a Roaming Pass before you travel or once you arrive to stay in control of your roaming usage. Keep data roaming turned off until your pass is active.

If you travel frequently, check out our Monthly Roaming Passes that keeps you roaming ready every month without needing to repurchase.

Tip: Turn off voicemail before you travel to avoid extra charges. Learn how.

What happens if I turn on data roaming without a Roaming Pass?

A Daily Roaming Pass will be automatically activated for you at RM39, with up to 2GB High-speed Internet.

How will I know when my Roaming Pass is expiring?

You'll receive a notification 3 hours before it expires, so you can renew or manage your usage.

Do I need to select a network when I arrive?

No need to worry! Your phone will automatically connect to the best partner network at your destination.

Having trouble browsing while roaming? Check our troubleshooting guide for help.

How do I contact CelcomDigi customer service while overseas?

While roaming overseas, you can reach our 24-hour customer support team by dialling *125# from your phone or contacting us via WhatsApp for assistance.

What is the 2026 School Holiday Grab Voucher Promo?

The 2026 School Holiday Grab Voucher Promo is a limited-time campaign where eligible postpaid customers can enjoy an RM30 Grab Voucher when they purchase a roaming pass for their principal line and at least one supplementary line. The promotion runs from 22 May 2026 to 7 June 2026. Learn more.
