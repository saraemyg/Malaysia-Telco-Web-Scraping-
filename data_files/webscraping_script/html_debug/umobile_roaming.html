Can the 24-Hour Roaming Passes be used in 2 different countries?

Yes it can. The 1GB High-speed Roaming Data is valid to be used in 2 different countries within 24 hours based on the respective passes.

 

For example:

You landed at Singapore Changi Airport at 11am and are waiting for your flight transfer to Australia at 4pm. Upon turning on Data Roaming, the 24-Hour Roaming RM15 - Auto will be provisioned to you. You will be able to use this 24-Hour Roaming Pass, with 1GB High-speed Roaming Data, in both countries.

What is International Roaming about?

International Roaming is a service that allows you to do the below when you are overseas:

  1. Access to high-speed roaming data with local operators
  2. Make & receive voice calls
  3. Send & receive SMS/MMS
What are the rates for roaming calls / SMS / data charges?

The charges are based on the countries and operators you have selected. For the full list, please click here.

Are there any peak/off-peak hours to make an international call?

Yes, charges would vary for peak and off-peak hours according to our roaming partner’s charges.
