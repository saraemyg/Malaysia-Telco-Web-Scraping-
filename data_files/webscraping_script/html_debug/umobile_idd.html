Make International Calls In A Few Simple Steps

How to Make Calls Using IDD 00

Dial 00 before the number you want to call.

00 <Country Code> <Area Code> <Mobile or Fixed Line Number>

Example of IDD 00 call to Indonesia​

00 62 12 3456789

How to Make Calls Using Best IDD 1310

Dial 1310 before the number you want to call.

1310 <Country Code> <Area Code> <Mobile or Fixed Line Number>

Example of Best IDD 1310 call to Indonesia

1310 62 12 3456789

How To Activate IDD via MyUMobile App

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Best IDD 1310?

1310 is a prefix that enables you to make IDD calls at lower rates.

Are the Best IDD 1310 rates applicable to video call, SMS or MMS?

The Best IDD 1310 rates are applicable only to voice calls. Please refer to our website for the rates, or check with our friendly customer service representative for further information.

What is the difference between IDD calls and Best IDD 1310?

Best IDD 1310 is a service that U Mobile offers to customers. Enjoy lower rates when you dial 1310 before dialling the country code, area code and phone number.
